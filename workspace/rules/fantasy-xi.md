# Fantasy XI Quick Rules

Pick exactly 11 unique `player_id` values from `game-board/players.json`.
Every selected player must be eligible for the current `matchday_id`; do not invent players, names, or IDs.
Use a legal roster shape: exactly 1 goalkeeper, 3 to 5 defenders, 3 to 5 midfielders, and 1 to 3 forwards.
There is no budget, captain, bench, or substitution step, so the submitted 11 players are the full scoring lineup.
Prefer likely starters and useful scoring opportunities, but validity comes first because an invalid Fantasy XI scores 0.
