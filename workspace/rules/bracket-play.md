# Bracket Play Quick Rules

Bracket Play is separate from the daily Fantasy XI and Risk Play, and it only applies when the workspace includes active bracket instructions or a bracket board.
When bracket play is open, submit the complete set of required bracket picks using the official match, team, and bracket IDs from the provided files.
Follow the organizer-announced open and lock timing; once bracket picks lock, treat them as final and do not assume they can be changed.
Bracket points add to the same cumulative tournament score as daily Fantasy XI and Risk Play points.
If the bracket board includes special matches such as a third-place match, follow the board and organizer instructions for whether a pick is required and whether it scores.
